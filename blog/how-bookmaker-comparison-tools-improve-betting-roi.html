Example: Finding Guaranteed Profits

Let’s break it down with a simple example. Imagine a boxing match where one sportsbook offers 2/1 odds on Fighter John, while another offers 2.5/1 odds on Fighter Paul. By betting $100 on John and $80.89 on Paul, you guarantee a profit regardless of the outcome:

  • If John wins: You earn $200 in profit, resulting in a $19.11 gain.
  • If Paul wins: You pocket $202.22 in profit, giving you a $21.33 gain.

This strategy, known as arbitrage betting, typically delivers modest returns - around 1.2% per opportunity. However, with odds updating every two seconds, you’ll need to be quick to capitalize on these fast-moving markets. Even small gains can add up significantly over time when executed consistently.
