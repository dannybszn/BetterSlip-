Setting Up Your First Alerts

  1. Access Alert Settings: Navigate to Settings > Alerts & Notifications in your dashboard
  2. Choose Alert Types: Enable arbitrage, positive EV, or other opportunity types
  3. Set Thresholds: Configure minimum profit percentages and bet amounts
  4. Select Sports: Choose your preferred sports and leagues
  5. Configure Delivery: Set up email, SMS, or push notification preferences
  6. Test Alerts: Use the test feature to verify alert delivery
  7. Monitor & Adjust: Fine-tune settings based on your experience
Pro Tip: Start with conservative settings (higher profit thresholds) and gradually become more aggressive as you gain experience and confidence with alert execution.
