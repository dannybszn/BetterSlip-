Frequently Asked Questions

üöÄ Getting Started

How much money do I need to start arbitrage betting?

Recommended minimum: $500-1,000

We recommend starting with at least $500-1,000 spread across 4-5 sportsbooks. This allows you to access most arbitrage opportunities while maintaining proper bankroll management. You can start smaller, but you'll have fewer opportunities available.

How quickly will I see profits?

Profits are immediate with arbitrage betting

Arbitrage profits are guaranteed regardless of game outcomes. Most users cover their subscription costs within the first week. Typical first-week profits range from $50-300 depending on bankroll size and time commitment.

Do I need to watch the games I bet on?

No, you profit regardless of outcome

With arbitrage betting, you profit no matter who wins because you're betting all possible outcomes at favorable odds. You never need to watch games or worry about specific results.

Is sports betting legal where I live?

Legal in 30+ US states and growing

Sports betting is legal in most US states and many countries worldwide. BetterSlip automatically filters opportunities based on your location. Check your local laws for specific regulations.

üéØ Using BetterSlip Tools

How accurate are the arbitrage opportunities?

99%+ accuracy with real-time verification

Our system scans odds every few seconds and verifies opportunities before displaying them. While odds can change quickly, our accuracy rate exceeds 99% for opportunities that remain available for 30+ seconds.

Why do some opportunities disappear quickly?

Arbitrage opportunities are time-sensitive

Sportsbooks constantly adjust odds based on betting activity. Arbitrage opportunities can last anywhere from seconds to hours. Acting quickly is essential for success.

What's the difference between arbitrage and positive EV betting?

Arbitrage guarantees profit, EV provides long-term edge

Arbitrage betting guarantees profit regardless of outcome by betting all sides. Positive EV betting identifies undervalued bets with long-term profit potential but requires variance tolerance.

How does the AI Parlay Builder work?

Machine learning finds correlated betting opportunities

Our AI analyzes millions of historical games to identify correlations between different betting outcomes, creating parlays with higher win probability than random combinations.

üè¶ Sportsbook Management

What happens if a sportsbook limits my account?

Account limits are manageable with proper strategy

Successful bettors may face account limitations. We provide strategies to minimize this risk and help you manage multiple accounts effectively. Most users maintain access to sufficient opportunities.

Which sportsbooks should I prioritize?

Start with major operators for maximum opportunities

Begin with DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, and Caesars. These provide the highest volume of arbitrage opportunities. Add regional books gradually to expand your options.

How do I manage bankroll across multiple sportsbooks?

Distribute funds based on opportunity frequency

Allocate 60% to major sportsbooks, 30% to secondary books, and keep 10% in reserve. Rebalance weekly based on where you're finding the most profitable opportunities.

What if a sportsbook voids my bet?

Rare but manageable situation

Bet voids are uncommon with major sportsbooks. If it happens, contact the sportsbook immediately and use our hedge calculator to determine if you need additional bets to maintain profit.

üîß Technical Issues

Why am I not receiving alerts?

Check notification permissions and settings

Verify that notifications are enabled in your device settings and BetterSlip app. Check your alert preferences in account settings and ensure your filters aren't too restrictive.

The mobile app is running slowly. What should I do?

Clear cache and restart the app

Close other apps, clear the BetterSlip app cache, and restart. If problems persist, update to the latest app version or contact support for assistance.

Why can't I connect my sportsbook account?

Check account credentials and permissions

Verify your sportsbook login credentials are correct. Some sportsbooks require explicit permission for third-party connections. Contact support if connection issues persist.

My dashboard isn't loading properly. How do I fix this?

Try browser troubleshooting steps

Clear your browser cache, disable ad blockers temporarily, and try a different browser. Most loading issues are resolved by clearing cache and cookies.
