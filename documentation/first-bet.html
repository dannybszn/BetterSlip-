Step-by-Step First Arbitrage

1. Find Your First Opportunity

  1. Open Arbitrage Finder: Navigate to the Arbitrage tool in your BetterSlip dashboard
  2. Filter for Beginners: Set minimum profit to 3%+ and maximum bet to $100 for your first attempt
  3. Choose Simple Markets: Start with moneyline or spread bets, avoid complex props
  4. Verify Opportunity: Look for opportunities with 30+ minutes until game time
‚è∞ Time Sensitivity: Arbitrage opportunities can disappear quickly as odds change. Once you identify an opportunity, act within 2-3 minutes for best results.

2. Example First Arbitrage

üèÄ Sample Opportunity: Lakers vs Warriors

  • Game: Los Angeles Lakers vs Golden State Warriors
  • Market: Point Spread
  • Bet 1: Lakers +3.5 at DraftKings (-110) - Bet $110
  • Bet 2: Warriors -3.5 at FanDuel (+105) - Bet $100
  • Total Investment: $210
  • Guaranteed Return: $220
  • Profit: $10 (4.76% return)

3. Calculate Bet Amounts

  1. Use BetterSlip Calculator: Click on the opportunity to see exact bet amounts
  2. Verify Math: Double-check calculations with our arbitrage calculator
  3. Round to Nearest Dollar: Most sportsbooks don't accept cents
  4. Confirm Profit: Ensure profit margin meets your minimum threshold

4. Execute the Arbitrage

üì± Execution Strategy

  1. Open Both Sportsbooks: Have both sportsbook apps/websites ready
  2. Verify Odds: Confirm odds haven't changed before placing bets
  3. Place First Bet: Start with the larger bet amount (usually the favorite)
  4. Place Second Bet: Immediately place the second bet
  5. Screenshot Confirmation: Save bet slip confirmations for your records

‚ö° Speed Tips

  • Stay Logged In: Keep sportsbook accounts logged in during betting hours
  • Save Payment Methods: Have payment methods saved for quick deposits
  • Practice Navigation: Familiarize yourself with each sportsbook's interface
  • Use Mobile Apps: Mobile apps are often faster than websites
