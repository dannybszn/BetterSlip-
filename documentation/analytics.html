Key Performance Metrics

üíµ Financial Performance

  • Total Profit/Loss: Overall financial performance across all betting activities
  • Return on Investment (ROI): Percentage return on total amount wagered
  • Profit Margin: Average profit per bet and per betting session
  • Bankroll Growth: Track bankroll evolution over time with growth rate analysis
  • Net Worth Impact: Total betting impact on overall financial position

üìä Betting Volume Statistics

  • Total Bets Placed: Count of all bets across different strategies
  • Average Bet Size: Mean stake amount and betting frequency patterns
  • Active Days: Frequency of betting activity and consistency metrics
  • Win Rate: Percentage of successful bets by strategy type
  • Strike Rate: Success rate for different confidence levels and EV thresholds

üé≤ Risk Analysis

  • Maximum Drawdown: Largest peak-to-trough decline in bankroll
  • Volatility Metrics: Standard deviation of returns and risk-adjusted performance
  • Sharpe Ratio: Risk-adjusted return measurement
  • Value at Risk (VaR): Potential loss estimates at different confidence levels
  • Kelly Criterion Adherence: Analysis of optimal vs. actual bet sizing
üìà Live Updates: All analytics update in real-time as you place bets, providing immediate feedback on performance changes and trend developments.
