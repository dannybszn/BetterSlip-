Player Correlations

One of the most powerful features of Fantasy Builder is understanding player correlations. If one player hits their prop, correlated players are more likely to hit too. For example:

  • Same Team Correlations: If a quarterback throws for over on passing yards, his receivers are more likely to hit over on receiving yards
  • Game Script Correlations: If a team is winning big, their running back is more likely to hit over on rushing yards
  • Opponent Matchups: If a defense is weak against the pass, multiple receivers from the opposing team may hit over

Fantasy Builder FAQ

How does Fantasy Builder calculate win probabilities?

We compare Fantasy/DFS lines to sharp sportsbook odds every minute. By analyzing millions of data points, we calculate the true win probability for each prop based on market consensus from sharp books like Pinnacle and Circa.

What do the colors mean?

Green indicates high-confidence plays with strong mathematical edge (typically 54%+ win probability). Yellow shows moderate edge opportunities. Focus on green plays for the best results, but you can mix in yellow to build larger slips while maintaining positive expected value.

How many picks should I put in my slip?

This depends on the platform. 2-3 pick slips are easier to hit but have lower payouts. 5-6 pick flex plays on PrizePicks offer 10-25x payouts but require ~55%+ hit rate per pick to be profitable. Our optimizer shows you which slip sizes are most profitable for each book.

Do I need sports knowledge to use this?

No! That's the beauty of data-driven betting. Just bet the plays highlighted in green and you're good to go. The math does the work‚Äîyou don't need to know anything about the players or teams.

How often is the data updated?

Our data refreshes every few seconds, so you always have the latest lines and projections. This ensures you never miss a line movement or new opportunity.
