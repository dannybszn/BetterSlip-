What are Fantasy Middles?

Fantasy Middles are opportunities where you can bet both sides of a player prop across different DFS platforms with a gap between the lines. If the result lands in the middle, you win both bets. Even if it doesn't, you've reduced your risk compared to betting one side.

Understanding Middle Size

The middle size is the gap between the two lines. For example:

  • 0.5 Gap: Over 25.5 vs Under 26.0 - Small gap, higher probability
  • 1.0 Gap: Over 25.5 vs Under 26.5 - Medium gap, balanced risk/reward
  • 2.0+ Gap: Over 25.5 vs Under 27.5 - Large gap, lower probability but bigger potential profit
Strategy Tip: Focus on middles with 1.0-2.0 gaps for the best balance of probability and profit potential. Smaller gaps are safer but less profitable, while larger gaps are riskier but offer bigger payouts.
