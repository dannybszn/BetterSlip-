What is Parlay Shopping?

Parlay shopping involves comparing the same multi-leg bet across different sportsbooks to find the highest potential payout. Unlike single bet line shopping, parlay odds can vary significantly between sportsbooks due to different correlation pricing and parlay calculation methods.

‚ö° Massive Payout Differences: The same parlay can have 10-30% payout differences between sportsbooks, especially on longer parlays with correlated legs.

Why Parlay Shopping Matters

üí∞ Significant Payout Differences

Example: 4-leg NFL parlay

  • Sportsbook A: +1200 odds ($100 bet wins $1,200)
  • Sportsbook B: +1450 odds ($100 bet wins $1,450)
  • Difference: $250 extra profit (20.8% increase)

üéØ Correlation Pricing Variations

Sportsbooks price correlated parlay legs differently:

  • Conservative Books: Reduce odds for correlated legs
  • Aggressive Books: Offer full parlay multiplication
  • Market Leaders: Most competitive parlay pricing
  • Recreational Books: Often inferior parlay odds

Types of Parlay Variations

üéØ Standard Parlays

  • Traditional Multiplication: Each leg's odds multiplied together
  • Independent Legs: Uncorrelated betting selections
  • Cross-Sport Parlays: Legs from different sports/leagues
  • Same-Game Exclusions: Multiple bets from single game

üìà Same-Game Parlays (SGP)

  • Correlation Adjustments: Reduced odds for related outcomes
  • Popular Markets: Player props + game totals
  • Sportsbook Specific: Each book has different SGP rules
  • Higher Variance: More dramatic pricing differences

üé≤ Parlay Boosts & Promos

  • Enhanced Odds: Temporary payout increases
  • Insurance Offers: Refunds if one leg fails
  • Profit Boosts: Percentage increases on winnings
  • Early Payout: Win if team leads by certain amount

Parlay Pricing Factors

üßÆ Mathematical Calculation Methods

  • True Odds Multiplication: Pure mathematical approach
  • Hold Adjustment: Increased house edge on parlays
  • Correlation Discounting: Reduced payouts for related bets
  • Market Competition: Competitive pressure on pricing

üìä Sportsbook Pricing Strategies

  • Volume Leaders: DraftKings, FanDuel offer competitive parlay odds
  • Market Makers: Sharp books with efficient pricing
  • Recreational Focused: Higher holds but better promotions
  • Specialists: Books specializing in certain sports/markets
Pro Tip: Same-game parlays often have the biggest pricing differences between sportsbooks. A single parlay can vary by 25-40% in payout between different books.
