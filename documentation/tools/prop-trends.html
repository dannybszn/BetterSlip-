What are Prop Trends?

Prop trends track how consistently a player hits certain statistical thresholds over their recent games. For example, if a player has scored over 20 points in 8 of their last 10 games, that's an 80% hit rate trend. This historical data helps you make more informed decisions on prop bets.

Understanding Hit Rates

üìä How Hit Rate is Calculated

Hit rate is calculated by looking at a player's recent games and counting how many times they exceeded a specific stat line. If a player hit over 7.5 rebounds in 7 out of 10 games, their hit rate for that line is 70%.

  • Higher hit rates (70%+) indicate more consistent performance at that level
  • Lower hit rates (40-60%) indicate more volatile or inconsistent performance
  • Very low hit rates (under 40%) may indicate the line is set too high for that player

üéØ What Makes a Good Prop Trend?

  • Consistent Hit Rate: 70%+ hit rate over last 5-10 games
  • No Recent Drop-Off: Hitting in recent games, not just older ones
  • Favorable Matchup: Playing against a team that allows the stat
  • Good Odds: Reasonable juice that doesn't eliminate the edge
Pro Tip: Look for trends where a player has hit 7+ times in their last 10 games. This indicates consistent performance, not just a hot streak that may not continue.

Best Practices

  1. Don't Chase Streaks: A high hit rate should be consistent, not just a recent hot streak.
  2. Check Matchups: Verify the player isn't facing an unusually tough opponent for that stat.
  3. Monitor Injuries: Check if key teammates are out, which can affect volume.
  4. Compare Multiple Lines: Sometimes a slightly different line offers much better value.
  5. Use Best Odds: Always place your bet at the sportsbook with the best odds.
