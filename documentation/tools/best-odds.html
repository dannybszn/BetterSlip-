What is Line Shopping?

Line shopping is the practice of comparing odds across multiple sportsbooks to find the best possible price for your bet. Even small differences in odds can significantly impact your long-term profitability when compounded over hundreds of bets.

‚ö° Sub-Second Updates: Our platform monitors odds changes across 50+ sportsbooks every second, ensuring you never miss the best available lines.

Why Line Shopping Matters

Consider betting $100 on a team at different odds:

  • Sportsbook A: +150 odds = $150 profit
  • Sportsbook B: +160 odds = $160 profit
  • Difference: $10 extra profit (6.7% increase)

Over 100 similar bets, this difference becomes $1,000 in additional profits!
