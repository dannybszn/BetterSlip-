What is Bet Tracking?

Bet tracking is the systematic recording and analysis of all your sports betting activity. It provides crucial insights into your betting performance, helps identify profitable strategies, and enables data-driven decision making for long-term success.

‚ö° Comprehensive Analytics: Our Bet Tracker automatically syncs with all major sportsbooks and integrates with every BetterSlip tool to provide complete performance visibility.

üéØ Strategy Optimization

How to Use Bet Tracker

  1. Connect Sportsbooks: Link your sportsbook accounts for automatic bet importing.
  2. Set Unit Size: Define your standard betting unit for consistent tracking.
  3. Review Auto-Imported Bets: Verify automatically imported bets are categorized correctly.
  4. Add Manual Bets: Input any bets from sportsbooks not connected.
  5. Analyze Performance: Review detailed analytics and identify trends.
  6. Optimize Strategy: Adjust betting approach based on data insights.

Performance Metrics

üéØ Core Metrics

  • Total Profit/Loss: Absolute dollar amount won or lost
  • ROI Percentage: Return on investment as a percentage
  • Unit Profit: Profit measured in betting units
  • Win Rate: Percentage of bets that won
  • Average Odds: Mean odds of your bets
  • Total Volume: Amount wagered across all bets

üìà Advanced Metrics

  • Closing Line Value (CLV): How often you beat final odds
  • Expected Value: Theoretical profit based on bet quality
  • Actual vs. Expected: Performance vs. statistical expectations
  • Sharpe Ratio: Risk-adjusted returns
  • Maximum Drawdown: Largest losing streak
  • Confidence Intervals: Statistical ranges for performance
Pro Tip: Focus on Closing Line Value (CLV) as much as immediate profits. Consistently beating closing lines indicates long-term skill and predictive edge.
