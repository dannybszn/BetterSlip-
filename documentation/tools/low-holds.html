What is a Hold?

The "hold" (also called vig or juice) is the built-in profit margin that sportsbooks include in their odds. It represents the mathematical advantage the house has over bettors. Lower holds mean better value for bettors and more efficient use of your bankroll.

Hold Calculation Example

NFL Game: Team A vs Team B

  • Team A: -110 (52.38% implied probability)
  • Team B: -110 (52.38% implied probability)
  • Total Implied Probability: 104.76%
  • Hold: 4.76% (the sportsbook's edge)
