Tool-Specific Filters

üéØ Arbitrage Finder Filters

  • Minimum Profit Percentage: Set your threshold (typically 2-10%)
  • Maximum Bet Amount: Limit opportunities to your bankroll capacity
  • Sportsbook Selection: Choose which books you have accounts with
  • Market Types: Moneylines, spreads, totals, props
  • Game Timing: Games starting within specific timeframes
  • League Restrictions: Focus on familiar leagues and competitions

üìà Positive EV Filters

  • Minimum EV Percentage: Threshold for positive expected value (3-8% recommended)
  • Model Confidence Level: Filter by AI model certainty
  • Bet Type Preferences: Player props, game totals, spreads, moneylines
  • Market Efficiency: Choose between sharp and recreational book opportunities
  • Volume Requirements: Minimum bet limits available
  • Closing Line Considerations: Expected line movement direction

ü§ñ AI Parlay Builder Preferences

  • Number of Legs: Preferred parlay size (2-8 legs typically)
  • Correlation Strength: Minimum correlation threshold
  • Risk Level: Conservative, balanced, or aggressive parlay construction
  • Same-Game Parlays: Enable/disable SGP suggestions
  • Cross-Sport Parlays: Multi-sport combination preferences
  • Anchor Bet Integration: Build around specific preferred bets

üé≤ Middles Finder Settings

  • Minimum Middle Range: Preferred point spread between books
  • Key Number Focus: Prioritize middles involving key numbers (3, 7 in NFL)
  • Maximum Risk Amount: Total exposure limit per middle opportunity
  • Hit Probability Threshold: Minimum expected middle success rate
  • Market Maturity: Early lines vs. closing line middle preferences
‚ö° Filter Balance: Too restrictive filters may limit opportunities, while too broad filters can create information overload. Start conservative and gradually expand.

üèÜ Team and Player Filters

Setting Up Your Preferences

  1. Access Settings: Navigate to Settings > Filters & Preferences
  2. Configure Bankroll: Set your total bankroll and unit size
  3. Choose Sports: Select your primary sports and leagues
  4. Set Risk Tolerance: Define your comfort level for different strategies
  5. Customize Tool Filters: Configure each tool based on your needs
  6. Link Sportsbooks: Connect your sportsbook accounts
  7. Test Configuration: Verify settings work as expected
  8. Refine Over Time: Adjust based on experience and results
Pro Tip: Save multiple filter profiles for different betting strategies (conservative, aggressive, specific sports) and switch between them based on market conditions.
